Known bugs Samsung Galaxy Tab 2

General Android 6.0 bugs

    Audio stuttering on some games:

    • To fix it you will have to use a root explorer and delete system/etc/permissions/android.hardware.audio.low_latency.xml (well, this isn't a proper fix but a good workaround until it is fixed properly

AOSP specific bugs

    No hwrotation

    • No hwrotation (meaning the boot animation is in landscape instead portrait on 7inch Tab2). We don't consider this as a problem, as autorotation will take effect after bootup anyways and most 7" tablets (like the Nexus 7) work this way as well.
